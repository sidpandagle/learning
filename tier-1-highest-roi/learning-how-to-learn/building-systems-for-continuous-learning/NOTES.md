# Teaching Notes

## User Profile
- Complete beginner to learning systems — has not tried Anki, Zettelkasten, Feynman technique, or any formal method
- Motivation is broad: wants to learn and grow generally, not tied to a specific domain
- No constraints on time, budget, or format
- Prefers format not yet established — will calibrate based on engagement with lessons

## Preferences to watch for
- User gave brief answers to mission questions — may prefer doing over theorising. Keep lessons action-oriented.
- "All of the above" response to goals suggests they want a complete system, not just one technique. Build toward a full system progressively.

## Teaching sequence rationale
1. Memory fundamentals first (the forgetting curve, active recall) — gives the "why" behind every technique
2. Habit building next — makes the system sustainable
3. Knowledge management after — once they know how to learn, show them how to organise it
4. Prioritisation and ROI — higher-order skill, needs earlier foundations first
