## What was established
User's primary research challenge is cutting through online noise quickly — too many unreliable or low-quality sources, unsure which to trust.

## Key context
- Focus: online research, not academic databases
- Practical use case: finding reliable answers to work and learning questions
- Not interested in deep academic research methodology

## Zone of proximal development
Start with: why the internet is noisy by design → how to evaluate a source quickly → the SIFT method (Stop, Investigate the source, Find better coverage, Trace claims back to origin).

Do not start with: Google Search operators, database research, or synthesis across many sources. Teach source evaluation first.
