# Mission: Effective Note-Taking

## Why
Notes currently don't exist as a system — ideas are captured randomly or not at all, and nothing is retrievable later. The goal is to build a consistent note-taking habit where the act of noting something actually helps you understand and remember it, not just record it.

## Success looks like
- Notes that you can actually find and use weeks later
- A consistent method that works across books, videos, conversations, and courses
- Notes that capture your own thinking, not just copied words
- A workflow that takes seconds to start and feels natural to maintain

## Constraints
- No prior system to migrate from — starting from scratch
- Should integrate with the broader continuous learning system from the parent workspace

## Out of scope
- Building a second brain or PKM mega-system (Obsidian, Roam, Notion deep dives)
- Academic citation and bibliography management
