# Mission: Reading Comprehension

## Why
Reading doesn't stick — the ideas feel clear in the moment but are almost entirely gone within a day. The goal is to shift from passive reading (moving eyes across words) to active reading (genuinely processing and retaining what matters).

## Success looks like
- Finishing a book or article and being able to summarise the key ideas without looking
- Knowing *how* to read different types of content (dense non-fiction vs. narrative vs. technical)
- Being able to identify the core argument of any piece in under a minute
- Retaining ideas long enough to actually apply them

## Constraints
- No specific genre focus — applies to books, articles, documentation, and long-form content
- Needs to work alongside the note-taking and spaced repetition systems from sibling workspaces

## Out of scope
- Speed-reading techniques that sacrifice comprehension for pace
- Academic close reading or literary analysis
