# Mission: Building Systems for Continuous Learning

## Why
To become someone who learns intentionally and consistently — turning curiosity into compounding skill across any domain. Right now learning feels unstructured; the goal is to build a personal system that makes growth automatic rather than accidental.

## Success looks like
- Having a daily or weekly learning routine that actually sticks
- Knowing *which technique to use* for different kinds of learning (facts, skills, concepts)
- Being able to pick up any new subject efficiently and retain it long-term
- Not feeling overwhelmed by "too much to learn" — having a system to prioritize

## Constraints
- None stated — open schedule, no budget restrictions, no prior system to migrate from

## Out of scope
- Topic-specific deep dives (this workspace is about HOW to learn, not WHAT to learn in a specific domain)
- Academic research methodology or formal education structures
