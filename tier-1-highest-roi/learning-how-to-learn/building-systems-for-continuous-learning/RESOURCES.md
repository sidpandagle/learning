# Building Systems for Continuous Learning — Resources

## Knowledge

- [Book: *Make It Stick: The Science of Successful Learning* — Brown, Roediger, McDaniel (Harvard University Press)](https://www.hup.harvard.edu/file/feeds/PDF/9780674729018_sample.pdf)
  The most research-backed popular book on learning science. Use for: spaced practice, interleaving, retrieval practice, effortful learning. Primary reference for Lessons 1–2.

- [Shortform Summary: Make It Stick](https://www.shortform.com/blog/make-it-stick-book/)
  Fast overview of the book's key takeaways. Use for: quick orientation before going deeper.

- [Scott H. Young — Ultralearning](https://www.scotthyoung.com/blog/ultralearning/)
  Principles for aggressive self-directed learning. Use for: directness, drill design, project-based learning. Primary reference for lessons on skill acquisition.

- [Evidence for Active Recall and Spaced Repetition — Recallify](https://recallify.ai/evidence-for-active-recall-and-spaced-repetition/)
  Summary of the research evidence. Use for: citations in lessons, convincing yourself these techniques work.

- [Spaced Repetition in STEM — ERIC (peer-reviewed)](https://files.eric.ed.gov/fulltext/EJ1241511.pdf)
  Academic paper on spaced repetition effectiveness. Use for: authoritative citation when depth is needed.

- [Personal Knowledge Management System — ingestai.io](https://ingestai.io/blog/personal-knowledge-management-system)
  Practical guide to PKM. Use for: lessons on capturing and organising knowledge.

- [OPM: Learning Strategies for a Continuous Learning Environment](https://www.opm.gov/policy-data-oversight/human-capital-framework/reference-materials/leadership-knowledge-management/continuouslearning.pdf)
  Government framework for continuous learning culture. Use for: habit and environment design lessons.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [r/GetStudying](https://www.reddit.com/r/GetStudying/)
  Active community for study techniques and learning systems. Strong signal-to-noise ratio. Use for: technique troubleshooting, routine critique.

- [r/Ultralearning](https://www.reddit.com/r/Ultralearning/)
  Community around Scott Young's framework. Use for: project-based learning advice, accountability.

- [r/PKM (Personal Knowledge Management)](https://www.reddit.com/r/PKM/)
  Community around note-taking systems (Obsidian, Notion, Zettelkasten). Use for: tool selection, capture workflows.

## Gaps

- No high-quality free resource found yet on **prioritising what to learn** (the meta-question of ROI across learning topics). Needs further search.
- No resource yet on **measuring learning progress** quantitatively. To find.
